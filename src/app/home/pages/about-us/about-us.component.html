About Us

Meet the amazing team behind this project and find out more about how we work.

Healthcrum is India's first online super speciality clinic that offers end-to-end services. Healthcrum is a new age Health-Tech organisation. Working on improving the quality of healthcare services for the patients living in remote area and who want to avail health services at home.
Anybody can avail our services which are not different than the local clinic. Special care is provided who suffer a heart attack and related illnesses depends upon the severity of the health.

- Providing the affordable/reachable services to all
- One-stop solution of all healthcare and wellness needs
- Personalised assistance to each individual
